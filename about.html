Our Story

From Origins to Advancement

Welcome to FURAT

Forum for Unified Research & Advancements in Traditional Medicine

FURAT is a healthcare platform established by a collective of Unani medical professionals, dedicated to advancing holistic care, research, and awareness within traditional systems of medicine. Guided by our motto, “From Origins to Advancement,” we seek to thoughtfully integrate time-honored medical knowledge with the evolving needs of modern healthcare.

Our platform provides access to medical consultations, patient appointments, wellness counselling, and carefully curated health solutions rooted in established holistic principles. With a strong emphasis on individualized care, we strive to deliver structured and personalized health plans tailored to each patient’s needs.

Grounded in the principles of research, clinical responsibility, compassion, and continuous learning, FURAT is committed to building a credible and evolving healthcare ecosystem. Our aim is to contribute meaningfully to the accessibility, development, and global relevance of traditional medicine.

Forum for Unified Research and Advancement in Traditional Medicine. Committed to holistic care, research, and the advancement of Unani medicine.
